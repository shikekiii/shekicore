About Us

We were founded in 2024 by Shekinah Resurreccion, a student at the FEU Institute of Technology, and are capable of creating immersive experiences that span multiple platforms. Whether it's dynamic video production, captivating visual design, or ground-breaking interactive content.

Join us on our journey to create, innovate, and elevate. Welcome to the center of creativity. Welcome to KekiCore.
